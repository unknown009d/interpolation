Interpolation

Welcome to interpolation calculator, you can calculate the Newton Forward Method and Newton Backward method here easily.


Instructions :

Step 1 :
Select the Method for calculation

Step 2 :
Textbox will appear to put the values of X,Y

Step 3 :
Put comma , after one particular data.

Step 4 :
Make sure the number of entries are same.

Step 5 :
Enter the Destination Value (f)

Step 6 :
Click on the calculate button.
